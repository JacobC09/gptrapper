# GPT WRAPPER

### Turn random noise into a sick beat.

---

Here's the pitch: you tap on your desk. You hum a little melody. You snap your fingers, slap your knee, bang on a water bottle. Those sounds — the ones your body and your environment make — become a beat.

That's it. That's the app.

---

## The Problem With AI Music

Every AI music tool sounds the same. You type "lo-fi hip hop" and you get lo-fi hip hop. Technically correct. Completely soulless. None of it has ever touched a human hand.

We wanted to make something different. Something where *you* are the instrument.

---

## What It Does

1. **Record** — hit the button, make some noise. Tap, hum, clap, stomp. Anything.
2. **Label** — the AI analyzes your clips and asks: is this your kick drum? Your snare? Your melody?
3. **Describe** — tell it the vibe. "Boom bap." "Chill trap." "Make it weird."
4. **Listen** — it composes a beat, and plays it back using *your actual sounds* as the samples. Your tap is the kick. Your hum is the synth. Your voice is in there.

Export it as an MP3 and walk away with something that has never existed before and could only have come from you.

---

## Why It's Human

Most tools replace the human. This one starts with the human and builds outward.

The beat is made from sounds *your body made*, in *your room*, on *this specific day*. It's the most personal music software we've ever seen — and it runs entirely in your browser.

---

## Why It's Cool

The piano roll lights up in cyan and violet as your beat plays back. The waveform pulses while you record. The whole thing looks like the future feels.

Oh, and the name? We called it **GPT WRAPPER** because every VC at the hackathon was complaining about GPT wrappers.

This is a GPT wrapper. It goes hard.
